About

About me

I'm a Lead UX Researcher with over a decade of experience. My foundation is understanding people: what they need, how they behave, and how they relate to the technology around them. I pair deep qualitative and behavioural insight with rigorous quantitative methods, giving teams both the understanding and the confidence to act.

The part that matters most to me is impact. I use research as a strategic lever to help teams decide what to build, when to ship, when to stop, and where to invest. I match the method to the level of ambiguity, I know when research adds the most value, and I treat storytelling as part of the craft. I also build AI into the research workflow to raise the speed and quality of insight, without compromising rigour.

Across every domain, my aim is the same: to make research consequential. My work has informed product direction and strategy, sharpened UI and UX decisions, and helped teams make better products by grounding them in real user evidence. I work as a strategic partner rather than a service function, close to where decisions get made, not just where data gets gathered.
